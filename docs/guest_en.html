Labour market is in a critical moment.
If are still unoccopied, don't give up!.
If you can't go directly to an Employement Centre, you can use one of the many public/private web search portal.
The main difficulty with these web sites is that you must know the exact name of job: they are somehow "rigid".
OpenLabor is a flexible tool, person-oriented and not job-centered.
OpenLabor provides you with a search engin that don't stitch to the exact word but try to enlarge the search field to job offers that could still be interesting for you.
OpenLabor tries to help in an open and ethical way, with efficacy and without many frills:
  • uses open data published by Employement Centers of Province of Rome: public, validate and certificate data
  • hidden all details of job categories and subcategories and points directly to job names and qualification required
  • allows for geographical search
  • directs you to the Employement Center responsible for the job offer
Moreover, "OpenLabor Roma" for Android is designed to be used everywhere and at every moment to search for a job and allows you:
  • to receive a notification
  • to share the job offers with your frends
  • to contact directy the Employement Center
The complete version of OpenLabor will be still more powerful, allowing registered users to:
  • report new job offers to the system
  • commento job offers
  • search for a job using their complete profile
  • get suggestions about the training that could be needed or useful to get it
